The Digital Showroom

Our concept for the digital showroom is an interactive slider catalog.
The user is able to select and get informations on different products directly on the image.
This allow Norrmade to display their products in their context and give a feeling to the user of Norrmade's values/ philosophy.
It also showcase how several Norrmade product would fit in an interior.
